Our Story

Built for Business Owners, By Business Owners

BraniumX was born from a simple observation: small business owners are losing thousands of dollars every month to missed phone calls.

Whether it's a nail salon owner who can't answer while doing a client's nails, a plumber who's under a sink, or a dentist whose front desk is overwhelmed—the problem is the same. Customers call, no one answers, and they move on to a competitor.

We built BraniumX to solve this problem using cutting-edge AI voice technology. Our AI receptionist answers every call instantly, books appointments, handles common questions, and never takes a day off.

Today, we're proud to help hundreds of small businesses capture more customers and grow their revenue—without hiring additional staff.
